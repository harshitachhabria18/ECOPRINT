How It Works

From Idea to Wardrobe,
Without the Waste

EcoPrint reimagines the fashion supply chain — design it, personalise it, print it locally, and return it for recycling.

01
✏️

Design

Independent designers upload patterns and garments to our open marketplace. Every design is optimised for 3D printing.

02
🎨

Customize

Enter your measurements, pick your material, choose your colorway. Every piece is made exactly for you — no more sizing waste.

03
🏭

Local Print

Orders are routed to the nearest certified EcoPrint hub. Printed using bio-based filaments with near-zero emissions.

04
♻️

Recycle

When you're done, return the garment to any hub. Materials are 100% recovered and re-spooled for future prints.

Materials

Built from Earth,
Returned to Earth

Every thread is engineered for circularity — bio-based, recyclable, and kind to the planet.

PLA Bio-Filament

Corn-based biodegradable plastic. Compostable at end of life. Softer finish, ideal for casual wear.

Ocean PET

Filament recovered from ocean plastic waste. Durable and recyclable. Perfect for outerwear and accessories.

Hemp Composite

Natural hemp fibres blended with bio-resin. Breathable, antibacterial. Best for warm-weather garments.
